### Flat Lay Not Showing?

**Check These:**
1. ‚úÖ Does your outfit have at least 2 items?
2. ‚úÖ Do your items have valid image URLs?
3. ‚úÖ Are the images accessible (not placeholder images)?

**If Still Not Working:**
- The system gracefully falls back to grid view
- You can still see all your items individually
- Try regenerating the outfit

### Images Look Wrong?

**Common Issues:**
- **Items Too Large**: Some items may appear oversized - this is a known limitation
- **Background Visible**: Light backgrounds work best; dark backgrounds may show through
- **Positioning Off**: Layout is optimized for standard clothing items

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Why isn't my flat lay showing?**
A: Flat lays require items with valid images. Check that your outfit items have uploaded photos (not placeholders).

**Q: Can I customize the flat lay layout?**
A: Currently the layout is automatic based on item types. Custom layouts coming soon!

**Q: How long does it take to generate?**
A: Typically 2-5 seconds depending on the number of items.

**Q: Can I download my flat lay?**
A: Yes! Click the download button on the flat lay view.

**Q: Will this work on all my outfits?**
A: New outfits generated after this feature was deployed will have flat lays. Existing outfits won't have them automatically.

**Q: What if I don't like the flat lay?**
A: You can toggle to grid view at any time. The flat lay is just an enhancement!
