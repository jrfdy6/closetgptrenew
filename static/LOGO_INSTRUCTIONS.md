# Logo Instructions

## ChatGPT Plugin Logo Requirements

Your plugin logo should be placed at `static/logo.png` and will be served at:
`https://aiclone-production-32dc.up.railway.app/static/logo.png`

### Specifications:
- **Format**: PNG
- **Recommended Size**: 512x512 pixels
- **Maximum Size**: 1MB
- **Background**: Transparent or solid color
- **Content**: Should be clear and recognizable at small sizes

### Current Status:
A placeholder file exists at `static/logo.png`. Replace it with your actual logo image.

### Testing:
After uploading your logo, test it's accessible at:
```
https://aiclone-production-32dc.up.railway.app/static/logo.png
```

The logo URL is configured in `.well-known/ai-plugin.json`.
